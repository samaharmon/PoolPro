Cleanliness Report

Daily Pool Cleanliness Report

Complete this form at the end of each shift to document your facility's daily condition. Select your facility below, then upload the required photos and complete all applicable fields.

Facility Photos

Submit photos of the following facility areas. If your facility has multiple pools, some submissions require photos in EACH pool area.

Deck 2 required

Submit at least two photos (per pool) of the deck surrounding each pool at your facility.

Pool 2 required

Submit at least two photos (per pool) of the pool area. If your pool has a deep end, make sure to include a photo of it.

Skimmers 2 required

Submit at least two photos (per pool) of clean skimmer baskets.

Damaged Equipment optional

Submit photos of any damaged equipment at your facility (e.g., loose ladder or diving board steps, cracking or crooked diving boards, damaged furniture, loose railings, deteriorating lifeguard tubes or backboard equipment, loose or broken pool tiles, etc.).

Other Notes

Use this field for any questions, concerns, or additional comments about your shift or facility.

Other Information Collected by Pool Managers ONLY

Complete this section only if you are a pool manager. Record current chemical supply levels for your facility.

Bleach Barrels optional photo

Upload a photo of the bleach barrel(s) and record the estimated volume remaining.

Muriatic Acid

Record the estimated amount of muriatic acid remaining at your facility.

Shock / Granular

Record the estimated amount of shock or granular chlorine remaining at your facility.

CYA Levels

Enter a CYA reading (0–100) for each pool at the selected facility. Select a pool location at the top of this form first.
